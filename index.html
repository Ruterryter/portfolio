Error Code Poetry

I like words. I really like words. Sometimes the meaning of the words as well, but sometimes just the word, the taste of it. Since I started my journey to become a devoloper I got to aquintance to a lot of new words, and quite often marked in read. These error codes are sometimes frightening, more often challenging, in bad days terribly discouraging, but most days a good way to learn. I deciced to make my own little library of error codes and try to see the beauty in them instead of fearing them.
