My thoughts

March 2020

Stepping out of the comfort zone

This last few weeks of the Technigo bootcamp has been a challenge, not only to learn new skills but mostly to learn how to cope with the imposter syndrome and the feeling of being a “newbie” in this field. Being used to be the one people asks for questions and now be the one always asking others for help is a challenge, and way out of my comfort zone. But as the weeks goes by I realized that when you let go of your pride and ask for help that accelerate your learning and you can go on in a much faster pace than if you would sit all night trying to solve the problem by yourself.
